About Your Adventure Golf

Since opening our doors to the public in 1987, Your Adventure Golf has become a must-see destination for people all over the Dayton area. We have also gone through some changes over the years. Our staff is excited to be able to help you get out and have some fun! Whether you're planning a day trip with your family or a quirky past timed date, we give visitors of all ages the chance to enjoy themselves in a fun-filled, ever evolving environment. Swing by and see what we're up to!

Is there an age minimum for children?

+

There is no age limit, but please note that our course dos not allow access for strollers. Please note that children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

Children under 12 are not permitted to play the blue course after 6 PM.
